🌾 The Problem

Farmers often detect crop diseases too late, leading to up to 30% yield loss. Current apps only identify diseases after they appear — when damage has already begun.

  • ❌ Late detection increases crop loss
  • ❌ Limited awareness about early warning tools
  • ❌ Heavy reliance on manual inspection

🚀 Our Solution

We combine Digital Twin, AI, and AR overlays to predict plant diseases and nutrient issues before they occur — saving crops, time, and money for farmers.

  • 🌱 Early disease prediction with AI
  • 🛰️ Digital Twin model for crop health monitoring
  • 📱 AR overlay for real-time visualization
  • 💡 Actionable recommendations to farmers

🌟 Main Features

AI Disease Detection

Identify crop diseases early with advanced AI models trained on real crop data.
