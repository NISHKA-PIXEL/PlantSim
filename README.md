# 🌱 PlantSim – AI Digital Twin of a Plant  

## 📌 Project Description  
PlantSim is an AI-powered app that creates a **digital twin of plants** using photos, weather, and soil data.  
Unlike normal plant apps that only detect problems after they appear, PlantSim can **predict future risks** such as nutrient deficiency, fungal attack, or drought stress.  
With **AR overlays**, farmers can point their camera at the plant and see the areas most at risk, along with simple care tips.  

👉 In short: PlantSim is like a **doctor + fortune teller for plants**.  

---

## 🚩 Problem Statement  
Farmers often discover plant issues **too late**, leading to crop loss, high costs, and overuse of chemicals.  
Current apps are **reactive**, not predictive.  

**PlantSim solves this** by predicting risks **before they happen** and showing them visually on the plant.  

---

## 🔧 Technologies Used  
- **AI Vision (YOLO/Mask R-CNN):** Detects early disease spots on leaves.  
- **ML Prediction (LSTM):** Forecasts plant health for the next few days.  
- **AR (ARCore/ARKit):** Highlights risky areas directly on the plant.  
- **Python + Firebase:** For backend, storage, and real-time updates.  
- **Weather APIs:** Adds local weather data for more accurate predictions.  

---

## ⚡ Challenges Faced  
- Unclear photos make AI results less accurate.  
- Hard to predict future health with limited crop data.  
- Aligning AR overlays with real plants was tricky.  
- Many farmers have basic phones and low internet.  

---

## 🌍 Impact  
- Helps farmers **save crops and reduce losses**.  
- Reduces **chemical overuse** by giving early warnings.  
- Supports **sustainability** with smarter farming practices.
